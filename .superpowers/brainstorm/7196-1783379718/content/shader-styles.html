Morning Sun Shaft — Style Direction

Which visual feeling fits your game best? Click to select.

A — Soft & Dreamy

One wide feathered beam of warm amber light. Edges fully blurred, smooth falloff. Tiny drifting dust motes. Very gentle — barely noticeable, adds warmth without drama. Think slow, cozy morning.

B — God Rays

2–3 distinct angled light beams fanning out from the window edge, getting wider and fading as they cross the room. Brighter at the source, dissipating across the floor. More dramatic and visible.

C — Window Frame Cast

The window frame itself projects a light pattern — bright panes of light divided by the shadow of the window frame bars. Feels grounded and architectural. Like actual sunlight coming through a real window.
